Hi, I’m Rory

If I had to describe myself in one word it would be diligent. I am highly motivated to complete my tasks to the highest standard and consistency, guided by my ethos of ‘if you’re going to do it, you do it the best you can’. I am always trying to push myself to improve a little every day and build a positive environment around me to support those that I spend my time with, at home or at work.
